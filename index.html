COVID-19 situation in the Singapore.

Overview of the Global Situation

Singapore, although located near Wuhan, China where the COVID-19 outbreak originated, has implemented stringent measures to control the spread of the virus. Despite being an attractive tourist destination, it is important to note that Singapore has experienced occasional spikes in COVID-19 cases.
